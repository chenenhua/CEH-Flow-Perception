## 1. Executive Summary
## 1. 执行摘要

CEH-Flow-Perception is a real-time spatiotemporal field engine for dynamic risk perception and physically emergent avoidance behavior.

CEH-Flow-Perception 是一个面向动态风险感知与物理涌现避障行为的实时时空场引擎。

The project is designed to explore an engineering route in which dynamic obstacles are represented as evolving fields rather than only as discrete symbolic detections.

本项目探索一条工程路线，即将动态障碍表示为持续演化的场，而不只是离散的符号检测结果。

Instead of relying exclusively on the standard sequence of detection, prediction, planning, and control, CEH-Flow-Perception investigates a field-to-force-to-motion pipeline.

CEH-Flow-Perception 并不完全依赖传统的检测、预测、规划、控制顺序，而是探索场到力再到运动的链路。

This architecture is especially relevant for robotics, industrial safety, intelligent manufacturing, camera-based reactive systems, and research prototypes in autonomous navigation.

这一架构尤其适用于机器人、工业安全、智能制造、基于摄像头的反应式系统以及自主导航研究原型。

## 2. Problem Statement
## 2. 问题定义

Modern dynamic obstacle systems often struggle with the following issues:

现代动态障碍系统通常面临以下问题：

- they can become overly dependent on explicit object categories
- 它们可能过度依赖显式物体类别

- they may lose continuity under partial occlusion
- 在部分遮挡下可能丢失连续性

- they frequently separate perception from control too rigidly
- 它们往往将感知与控制切分得过于僵硬

- they often provide outputs that are difficult to interpret physically
- 它们输出的结果经常缺乏物理可解释性

A dynamic environment is not merely a list of objects.

一个动态环境并不只是一个物体列表。

It is a continuously evolving spatial medium in which motion, history, occupancy, uncertainty, and interaction all matter.

它是一个持续演化的空间介质，运动、历史、占据、不确定性和交互都很重要。

## 3. Core Technical Thesis
## 3. 核心技术主张

CEH-Flow-Perception models dynamic influence as a field with memory.

CEH-Flow-Perception 将动态影响建模为具有记忆的场。

At a simplified level, the engine combines a fast conduction field and a slower memory field.

在简化层面上，引擎将快速导通场与较慢记忆场结合起来。

The conceptual update is:

其概念更新形式为：

$$
C_t = \rho C_{t-1} - \delta + \alpha M_t + \lambda L_t
$$

$$
L_t = (1-\eta)L_{t-1} + \eta M_t
$$

Where:

其中：

- $M_t$ is a motion-derived input
- $M_t$ 是由运动导出的输入

- $C_t$ is the fast conduction field
- $C_t$ 是快速导通场

- $L_t$ is the slower learning or memory field
- $L_t$ 是较慢的学习或记忆场

The resulting potential produces local forces:

由此得到的势场产生局部力：

$$
\vec{F} = - \nabla V
$$

Agents do not simply wait for symbolic decisions.

智能体并不是等待符号式决策后再行动。

They move under the net effect of forces generated by the evolving field.

它们在演化场产生的合力作用下运动。

## 4. Architectural Layers
## 4. 架构分层

### 4.1 Sensor Layer
### 4.1 传感层

The sensor layer receives image streams, derives motion evidence, and projects motion evidence into field space.

传感层接收图像流，提取运动证据，并将运动证据投射到场空间中。

### 4.2 Field Layer
### 4.2 场层

The field layer updates the conduction field, memory field, and normalized potential field.

场层更新导通场、记忆场以及归一化势场。

### 4.3 Dynamics Layer
### 4.3 动力学层

The dynamics layer computes force, acceleration, velocity, and position for one or more agents.

动力学层计算一个或多个智能体的受力、加速度、速度和位置。

### 4.4 Feedback Layer
### 4.4 反馈层

The feedback layer applies agent back-reaction so that agents also reshape local field conditions.

反馈层对智能体施加反作用，使智能体本身也会重塑局部场条件。

### 4.5 Audit Layer
### 4.5 审计层

The audit layer outputs risk gradient, repulsion force, collision entropy, conductance memory, and trajectory-related diagnostics.

审计层输出风险梯度、排斥力、碰撞熵、导通记忆和轨迹相关诊断指标。

### 4.6 Presentation Layer
### 4.6 展示层

The presentation layer renders understandable Chinese UI for non-technical viewers and professional logs for engineers and researchers.

展示层向非技术观众呈现易懂的中文界面，同时向工程师和研究人员输出专业日志。

## 5. Why This Matters
## 5. 这为什么重要

A field-based system can preserve continuity where purely symbolic systems may become brittle.

基于场的系统在纯符号系统容易脆弱的地方，更有可能保持连续性。

If motion leaves a memory trace in the medium, the system can still carry residual risk information after the object itself becomes partially occluded or disappears.

如果运动在介质中留下记忆痕迹，那么即使目标本身部分遮挡或消失，系统仍然可以保留残余风险信息。

This is not just object detection.

这不只是目标检测。

It is a form of spatiotemporal continuity modeling.

它是一种时空连续性建模。

## 7. 可解释性优势

CEH-Flow-Perception 则可以暴露以下中间量：

## 9. Risk and Limitation
## 9. 风险与限制

CEH-Flow-Perception is an engineering framework and research direction, not a magical guarantee.

CEH-Flow-Perception 是一个工程框架与研究方向，而不是魔法保证。

Its performance depends on camera quality, scene structure, tuning quality, and deployment discipline.

它的表现依赖摄像头质量、场景结构、调参质量以及部署纪律。

Field methods can also fail if they are badly normalized, poorly tuned, or used outside validated scenarios.

如果归一化不好、调参很差，或者使用在未验证场景中，基于场的方法同样可能失败。

Therefore, commercial deployment should include calibration, benchmarking, safety review, and scenario validation.

因此，商业部署应包含标定、基准测试、安全复核和场景验证。
